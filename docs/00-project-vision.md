# 00 — Project Vision

---

## Product Name (Working Title)

**Twogether** — A shared life-planning platform for couples preparing for marriage.

---

## Product Vision

> *"The first platform where couples don't just manage money together — they build a life together."*

Twogether is a warm, modern, and deeply personal web application that helps engaged and pre-marriage couples align financially, plan their wedding, and grow toward a shared future — without feeling like a spreadsheet or an accounting tool.

It is the digital equivalent of sitting together at the kitchen table with two cups of coffee, reviewing your dreams and your numbers side by side.

---

## Mission

To reduce financial stress in relationships by giving couples a shared, transparent, and emotionally intelligent space to plan their wedding and life together — starting from the moment they say "yes."

---

## Core Philosophy

### 1. Finances Are Intimate

Money is one of the top causes of conflict in relationships. This product exists not just to track numbers, but to create *shared understanding*. The interface should feel like collaboration, not auditing.

### 2. Planning Is Romantic

Wedding planning and saving toward shared goals are not chores — they are acts of love and commitment. The product should celebrate milestones, not just report balances.

### 3. Transparency Without Surveillance

Both partners should feel informed, never monitored. The product must balance visibility with personal space. Each partner maintains individual autonomy while participating in a shared financial life.

### 4. Simplicity Over Completeness

This is not a full personal finance suite. It is a focused tool for a specific life stage. Features should be curated, not exhaustive. Every screen should feel purposeful.

### 5. Emotional Design Matters

Color, typography, micro-interactions, copy tone, and empty states all carry emotional weight. The product should feel warm and celebratory, not clinical or transactional.

---

## Product Principles

| # | Principle | Meaning |
|---|-----------|---------|
| 1 | **Couple-first** | Every feature is designed for two people, not just one |
| 2 | **Celebrate progress** | Show how far they've come, not just how far they need to go |
| 3 | **Reduce friction** | Logging an expense should take under 10 seconds |
| 4 | **Respect privacy** | Personal expenses stay personal unless shared |
| 5 | **Make money feel human** | Language, colors, and UI should feel warm — never cold |
| 6 | **Plan for real life** | Couples have different income levels, spending habits, and goals |
| 7 | **Be trustworthy** | Data security and financial accuracy are non-negotiable |

## Success Metrics

### Engagement Metrics
- Daily active couple rate (target: >40% of registered couples active weekly)
- Average expense logs per couple per week (target: >5)
- Checklist completion rate (target: >60% of checklist items completed before wedding date)
- Savings goal progress rate (% of couples who hit at least one milestone)

### Retention Metrics
- Couple retention at 30 days (target: >65%)
- Couple retention at 90 days (target: >45%)
- Both partners active within the same week (target: >55% of couples)

### Satisfaction Metrics
- Net Promoter Score (target: >50)
- "Makes me feel closer to my partner" sentiment in surveys (target: >70% agree)

### Business Metrics (Future)
- Conversion to premium tier (target: >15% of active couples)
- Referral rate (target: >20% of new signups from couple referrals)

## Long-Term Vision

**Year 1:** The go-to wedding planning + shared finance app for engaged couples in the target market.

**Year 2:** Expand beyond wedding — become a life-stage companion. New modules for post-wedding budgeting, home buying, family planning funds, and travel savings.

**Year 3:** Introduce financial wellness insights, personalized recommendations ("You're spending 30% more on dining out than similar couples saving for a home"), and optional third-party integrations (bank sync, e-wallet connections).

**Year 5:** Become the relationship's financial operating system — from engagement to retirement planning — trusted by couples at every life stage.

---

## What This Product Is NOT

- Not a replacement for a bank account
- Not a comprehensive personal finance app (not competing with Mint or YNAB)
- Not a wedding marketplace or vendor directory
- Not a social network or public platform
- Not a financial advisor

---

## Differentiation Hypothesis

Most finance apps are designed for *individuals*. Most wedding planning apps are designed for *logistics*. No product is designed for *couples building a financial foundation together*. Twogether occupies that white space — the intersection of financial transparency, emotional connection, and wedding preparation.

The differentiator is not the feature set. It is the **emotional experience** of using the product together.
